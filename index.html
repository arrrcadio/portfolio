About me aka short historical background

I dived into the world of UX back in 2013 after taking Stanford’s Human-Computer Interaction course (shoutout to professor Scott Klemmer!) and never looked back. My first UX job kicked off soon after, and since then, I’ve gone from a junior designer figuring things out to a senior product designer at a $5B global company.

I like to work directly with developers, analysts and product managers to achieve measurable business results.

I believe great design is data-informed, user-centered, and never just about making things pretty (though that doesn’t hurt).

When I’m not creating another awesome product, you’ll probably find me digging through vinyl records, hiking somewhere scenic, or writing about design.
